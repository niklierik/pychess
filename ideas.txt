Upgrades:
  White:
    Add a traditional piece to the board (Pawn(1), Rook(5), Bishop(3), Knight(3) or King(10))
    
    Fairy Chess Pieces cannot be made during promotion, only Traditional Pieces 
    (Queen included even tough it is created with the help of other pieces)

    Any derived piece can be used for "Craft" material, but the system should avoid these situations.

    The Queen(9):
      Replace a Rook and a Bishop with the Queen

    The Chancellor(7):
      Replace a Rook and a Knight with the Chancellor: Rook + Knight
    
    The Dragon King(15):
      Replace the King and a Rook with the Dragon King: Rook + King
    
    The Archbishop(5):
      Replace a Bishop and a Knight with Archbishop: Bishop + Knight
    
    God's Divinity:
      Bishop or pieces derived from Bishops may heal instead of moving.

    The Pope(20):
      Replace the King and a Bishop with the Pope: Bishop + King
    
    The Dragon Horse(25):
      Replace the King and a Knight with the Dragon Horse: Knight + King
    
    The Amazon Queen(10):
      Replace both Knight and the Queen with an Amazon: Knight + Queen

    The Nightrider(10):
      Replace two Knights as the Nightrider (Knight, but can repeate moves multiple times at the same direction)
    
    Camel(4):
      Replace a Knight as Camel: 3 + 1

    Zebra(5):
      Replace a Camel as Zebra: 2 + 3
    
    Giraffe(6):
      Replace a Zebra as Giraffe: 3 + 4

    The Queen of the Night(10):
      Replace the Amazon Queen and the Nightrider with the Queen of the Night: Nightrider + Queen

    The Silver General(2):
      Replace three pawns as Silver General (Can move forward or diagonally one square freely)

    The Gold General(4):
      Replace 2 Silver General as a Gold General (moves like the King)
    
    The Grasshopper(7):
      Replace a Knight and a Queen with a Grasshopper: Moves like the Queen but must jump above a piece (any color) and captures the piece on the other side

    The Fool(5):
      Replace three pawn as the Fool: Moves like the piece Black moved last.

    Medical Studies:
      Doubles start HP of pieces
